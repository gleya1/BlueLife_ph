Ways to Help Protect Our Water

Small actions can make a huge impact. Here’s what you can do to help preserve rivers, lakes, and oceans.

1. Reduce Plastic Use

Avoid single-use plastic items like straws, cups, and bags. Plastics often end up in oceans and harm marine life.

2. Join Clean-up Drives

Participate in community clean-up activities to remove trash from beaches, rivers, and streets.

3. Save Water at Home

Turning off the tap while brushing and taking shorter baths can save thousands of liters of water per year.

4. Dispose Waste Properly

Do not throw trash into rivers, canals, or drainage. Proper waste disposal prevents water contamination.

5. Support Eco-friendly Products

Choose biodegradable, environmentally safe products to reduce pollution.

6. Educate Others

Share knowledge about water conservation. Awareness inspires action!
